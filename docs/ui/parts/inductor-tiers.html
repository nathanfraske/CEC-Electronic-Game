INDUCTORAIR CORE

The inductor, across the three tiers

The same inductor shown three ways, switched with the tier control and wired as a simple energize and de-energize loop so you can watch it work. Tier 1 is the schematic: a supply, a series resistor, and the coil symbol, with current that ramps up instead of jumping because the coil resists any change. Tier 2 is the reality-analogous view: a heavy flywheel turned by the flow in a pipe, where the spin is the current, the pressure across the wheel is the voltage, the spinning momentum is the stored energy, and a heavier wheel is a larger inductance. Tier 3 is the reality-matched view: a real coil of wire whose current builds a magnetic field, where the field holds the energy and fights every change in current, and more turns means more inductance. All three run on one model, V = L dI/dt with energy half L I squared, the current follows L / R, and every value is a free parameter for the game to set.
