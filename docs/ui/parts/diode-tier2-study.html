Diode tier 2, two ways to picture it

Two candidate analogies for the same diode, both driven by the real Shockley junction equation with a 100 ohm series limiter, so the motion is honest. Spring valve is the hydraulic picture, a spring loaded ball that lifts off its seat once the forward push beats the spring. Gravity hill is the barrier picture, a crowd of charge balls held back by a hill that the voltage raises and lowers. Flip between them and sweep the voltage from reverse to forward. The type buttons change the threshold.
