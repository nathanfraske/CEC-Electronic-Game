TRANSFORMERIRON CORE

The transformer, across the three tiers

The same transformer shown three ways, switched with the tier control and driven with AC so you can watch power cross from one winding to the other. Tier 1 is the schematic, an AC source on the primary, a shared core, and a secondary feeding a load, with the two sides never touching. Tier 2 is the reality-analogous view, two wheels linked by a belt, where the primary flow spins one wheel and the belt drives the other against its own load, and the wheel sizes set the ratio. Tier 3 is the reality-matched view, two windings on a shared iron core, where the primary current drives a changing flux around the core and that changing flux induces the secondary voltage. Across all of it the turns ratio sets the voltage ratio, the current ratio runs the other way so power is conserved, and the coupling is magnetic only, which is why the two circuits stay isolated. Every value is a free parameter for the game to set.
